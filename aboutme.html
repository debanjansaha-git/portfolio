About Debanjan
a.k.a Deb

Data Evangelist and Aspiring Machine Learning Engineer

Hello, I'm Debanjan Saha, a seasoned Data Architect with a decade of transformative experience in the realm of data management and engineering. My journey has been a dynamic blend of orchestrating end-to-end data solutions, specializing in the intricacies of AWS and Azure. As an adept architect, I've woven together the threads of compliance, performance optimization, and delivering impactful insights into a tapestry of successful projects.

From the heart of cutting-edge healthcare R&D at Nanobiosym Inc. to steering the ship of data innovation at Optiks Innovation India Limited, I have relentlessly pursued excellence. My endeavors include architecting scalable solutions for HIPAA-compliant Covid-19 trials, transforming unstructured data into FHIR format, and deploying predictive models with a keen eye on data drift detection.

In the labyrinth of Azure's healthcare landscape, I led the charge in engineering comprehensive end-to-end data solutions. My toolkit included Spark, Python, Databricks, and a dash of innovation, reducing data processing time by 40% and fortifying data governance to safeguard sensitive healthcare data.

During my tenure at Cognizant Technology Solutions, I donned the hat of a Senior Associate, crafting hybrid AWS-based client broker interfaces and pioneering real-time streaming pipelines. Witnessing a 65% boost in customer satisfaction and a 50% reduction in data processing time, the impact of data accuracy enhancements by an astounding 90% through reconciliation and transformation speaks volumes.

In the IT landscape, I've navigated the complexities of mainframe systems, seamlessly migrating large-scale data and codebases with minimal loss. My journey spans from Bengaluru to Kolkata, where I optimized mainframe data performance, contributing to a significant boost in overall system operation time.

My technical prowess extends across languages like Python and SQL, cloud platforms including AWS and Azure, big data technologies like Spark, and a rich array of databases from Redshift to Synapse and beyond. Proficient in data visualization tools like Power BI and Tableau, my machine learning expertise encompasses Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow, and Spark ML.

Within my portfolio, you'll find a showcase of projects reflecting my passion for innovation. From spearheading a GPT-4-powered Legal Aid Chatbot to leading a comprehensive analysis of retail transactions for customer profiling, each project represents a chapter in my journey of data-driven exploration.

Embark on this odyssey with me, where data isn't just about numbers; it's about weaving narratives that shape the future. Let's turn your data challenges into triumphs, crafting solutions that resonate with precision and impact. Welcome to the intersection of expertise, innovation, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.
